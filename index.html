Built by someone still on the job

Built For More isn't a finance influencer or a guy who left the job to sell you a course. It's run by an active-duty Florida firefighter with 15 years on the job — who also sat on a pension board, negotiated collective bargaining agreements, and taught benefits classes in the department.

That matters because your benefits aren't generic. Pension multipliers, DROP rules, and 457(b) options vary by state, plan, and contract. This is education from the inside — for firefighters, law enforcement, EMS, dispatch, and corrections.
